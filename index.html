How to play

You have 8 presents to deliver into 8 chimneys . The drop arrow moves left and right. Press the space bar or tap the screen release the present.

But beware: unless you can land a direct hit on the chimney, the present will bounce!

To win the game, you must deliver all 8 presents into unique chimneys.
